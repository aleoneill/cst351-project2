Practice Areas

Kennedy, Archer & Giffen’s highly experienced attorneys provide comprehensive legal services in a broad range of practice areas. The firm’s clients vary widely, and include major corporations, privately held businesses, insurance companies, investment partnerships, and entrepreneurs. Our team is robust enough to handle the most complex legal matters, but lean enough to provide efficient and cost-effective legal services. On the transactional side, we assist companies in a variety of industries with organizational and compliance matters, buying, selling and merging businesses, forming joint ventures, and general business planning as well as counseling regarding corporate and partnership operations. The firm's civil litigation group has experience in all types of business and individual litigation and appellate practice. We have handled business disputes of virtually every type, including cases arising from business transactions between small private companies to disputes involving large publicly-held corporations. These cases have included complex contract actions, employment law issues, construction defect allegations, medical malpractice and other professional liability defense, business interference claims, unfair competition, corporate and partnership disputes, product liability, and real estate matters.

PRACTICE AREAS

Kennedy, Archer & Giffen’s highly experienced attorneys provide comprehensive legal services in a broad range of practice areas. The firm’s clients vary widely, and include major corporations, privately held businesses, insurance companies, investment partnerships, and entrepreneurs. Our team is robust enough to handle the most complex legal matters, but lean enough to provide efficient and cost-effective legal services. On the transactional side, we assist companies in a variety of industries with organizational and compliance matters, buying, selling and merging businesses, forming joint ventures, and general business planning as well as counseling regarding corporate and partnership operations. The firm's civil litigation group has experience in all types of business and individual litigation and appellate practice. We have handled business disputes of virtually every type, including cases arising from business transactions between small private companies to disputes involving large publicly-held corporations. These cases have included complex contract actions, employment law issues, construction defect allegations, medical malpractice and other professional liability defense, business interference claims, unfair competition, corporate and partnership disputes, product liability, and real estate matters.

Our business lawyers counsel all types of business entities, including profit and nonprofit corporations, partnerships (both general and limited), limited liability companies, and joint ventures at many different stages in the business life cycle – from selecting the form of business entity and capitalizing and financing the organization, through mergers, acquisitions and dissolutions.

  • Organizing a number of non-profit organizations and obtaining tax exempt status; and

We provide counsel in a full range of M&A transactions involving small and large businesses at all stages of development. This may include counseling regarding transaction structure, finance, employee impact, success planning and more.

Kennedy, Archer & Giffen’s mediation practice is led by veteran attorney, Robert G. Simpson. Mr. Simpson’s practice coincides with a 44-year career as a civil litigator, enhanced by extensive training in mediation along with representing clients in hundreds of mediations. His unique perspective is a recognition of what clients are trying to achieve, and what is actually achievable for both sides. Mr. Simpson has a unique ability to analyze complex situations and assist parties at arriving at the very best solution to the problem from both perspectives. It is this desire to help resolve conflicts that brought Mr. Simpson to the field of mediation.

Mr. Simpson’s career spans decades of experience advocating on behalf of plaintiffs and defendants. During the first 22 years of his career, Mr. Simpson handled matters from the defense side of the bar, principally representing clients of insurance companies and private businesses, along with certain public entities. Mr. Simpson’s practice included defense of catastrophic injury and death cases, automobile accident, product liability, medical and legal malpractice, complicated business disputes and public entity liability. The next 18 years of Mr. Simpson’s career were devoted to the representation of injured persons in all fields of potential liability. In the course of that practice, Mr. Simpson obtained a number of verdicts in excess of $1,000,000 and settlements totaling over $500,000,000.

Since 2014, Mr. Simpson has worked with the firm of Kennedy, Archer & Giffen, primarily handling complex litigation representing both plaintiffs and defendants. Mr. Simpson’s latest successful trial was a multi-week affair in San Francisco involving a dispute between technology companies.
